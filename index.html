Serving Firms Since 1932

Started in 1932 by Ray Whimmer as The Overhead Door Company of Toledo, we are the oldest operating garage door company in Toledo, Ohio. We are proudly celebrating our 95th year serving the Toledo Area. Over the years of operation, the company has evolved and expanded to further serve its customers. In 1982, the owner at the time, Lee Huss, founded the fireplace division of the company originally designed to serve the builder community. Today, Fireside Hearth and Home of Toledo proudly sells industry leading products supported by our own factory trained and certified installers and service technicians.
